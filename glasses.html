Why did I create this project? I was presented with an opportunity to be involved in this research project. I was inspired to challenge myself and build the this project with my professor as part of a research as i have never done anything like this before. These Smart Glasses are made with the intention of learning and donating them to individuals with vision issues.

The glasses are supposed to help the user detect the objects in front of him by assisting him through a voice companion to direct his way, other functions are also being discussed for further implementation.

I will be responsible for taking care of the software part, where I will have to prepare the data to detect objects, annotate the image data for the regression process, and train a neural network on the data using python, AI and ML.
