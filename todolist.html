The title of my project

To Do List Project ✔️

So why was I inspired to create this app?

I was inspired to build my to-do list apps to increase productivity, and what it allows you to do is to create new to-do lists, for example, your travel to-do list, and you can select one of them to go into that particular list and add new items to it. For example, "Buy Apples," "Buy Pears." I can also check these items off, but I can also be able to swipe to delete them. There's also a search of your to-do list, for example, let's search for something like "Apple," hit search, and you see all the to-do list items that contain that word.

In addition to all of this and the best part is all the data will be stored and continued locally on my phone. So that means even if I go ahead and end my app or changed my phone, all of the data will be persisted on the device with no internet connection needed.
